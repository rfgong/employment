From all data to equation regressions:

1. merge_market.py
    Input:
        - compustat_full_month.csv
        - crsp_full_month.csv
    Output:
        - market_measures.csv

2. factor_alphas.py
    Input:
        - crsp_full_month.csv
        - factors3.csv
        - factors5.csv
    Output:
        - ff1alphas.csv
        - ff3alphas.csv
        - ff5alphas.csv

3. collect_skills.py
    Input:
        - cognism_current.txt
        - cognism_join.txt
        - cognism_leave.txt
    Output:
        - skills_current.csv
        - skills_join.csv
        - skills_leave.csv

4. find_premia.py
    Input:
        - ff1alphas.csv / ff3alphas.csv / ff5alphas.csv
        - market_measures.csv
        - skills_current.csv / skills_join.csv / skills_leave.csv
    Output:
        - tobit_ols.csv
        - premia.csv
        - premia_single_eq.csv
        - CSV files for Fama-Macbeth
